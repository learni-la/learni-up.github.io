Our purpose is to build the online platform accessible for Lao students - anytime anywhere.


Our core value is based on the unlimited-knowledge-seeking (life-long-learning) mindset of students who are willing to learn and become excellent in their desired field.


Our philosophy is that "We never stop improving our system for the better use of students all across the country".


Our single vision is to provide Lao students opportunities to develop their science knowledge.


Last but not least, our identity is "We are the symbol of the science-knowledge center of Lao students".
